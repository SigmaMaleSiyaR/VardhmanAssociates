Our Refund Policy

At Vardhman Associates, we strive to provide the highest quality professional services to our clients. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds of our services.

1. Service Completion

Once a service has been completed and delivered to the client, no refunds will be provided. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Completed business registrations
  • Filed tax returns
  • Completed legal documentation
  • Delivered advisory services

2. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be considered in the following circumstances:

  • If the service cannot be completed due to circumstances beyond our control
  • If the client provides incorrect information that prevents service completion
  • If the service is cancelled before substantial work has begun

3. Consultation Fees

Initial consultation fees are non-refundable. This includes:

  • Free 15-minute consultations
  • Paid detailed consultations
  • Strategy sessions

4. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Submit a written request to vardhman.assoiates8@gmail.com
  2. Include your client reference number and reason for refund
  3. Allow 5-7 business days for review
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed within 10 business days

5. Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy may be made at the discretion of management in cases of:

  • Service quality issues
  • Technical difficulties
  • Extenuating circumstances
