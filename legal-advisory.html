Professional Legal & Corporate Advisory

At Vardhman Associates, we provide comprehensive legal and corporate advisory services to help businesses navigate the complex regulatory landscape. Our team of experienced professionals combines legal expertise with business acumen to deliver practical solutions that protect your interests and support your growth objectives.

We understand that legal compliance is crucial for business success. Our services are designed to ensure that your business operations align with applicable laws and regulations while minimizing legal risks. From contract drafting to corporate compliance, we provide end-to-end support to help you make informed business decisions.

Contract Services
Expert drafting and review of various contracts including vendor agreements, employment contracts, and commercial agreements. We ensure your contracts are legally sound and protect your interests.
Legal Notices
Professional drafting of legal notices and responses, handling of disputes, and representation in arbitration proceedings. We help resolve conflicts efficiently while protecting your rights.
ROC Compliance
Comprehensive ROC compliance services including board meeting management, annual filings, and statutory compliance. We ensure your company meets all regulatory requirements.
Secretarial Services
Professional secretarial services including statutory audits, DIR reporting, and compliance monitoring. We help maintain proper corporate governance standards.

Our approach combines legal expertise with practical business understanding. We work closely with you to understand your business objectives and provide tailored solutions that address your specific needs. Whether you're starting a new business, expanding operations, or dealing with regulatory challenges, our team is here to guide you through every step of the process.
