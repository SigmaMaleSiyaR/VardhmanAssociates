CIVIL LAW

Protecting Your Rights, Resolving Your Disputes

In life, disputes may arise that affect your property, family, or finances. As your trusted civil law advocate, I help you navigate these complex legal issues with clarity, confidence, and commitment. Whether it's a property dispute, a family partition matter, or recovering your hard-earned money, my goal is to deliver timely and effective legal solutions that protect your rights and secure your interests.

Property Disputes

Client Perspective: "I want to protect my ownership rights and resolve land or house disputes legally and fairly."

We assist clients in resolving issues related to illegal possession, ownership claims, encroachments, sale deed conflicts, and boundary disputes. Our expertise ensures swift and lawful resolution through negotiation, mediation, or court proceedings.

Types:
  • Title disputes
  • Possession issues
  • Easement rights
  • Builder-buyer disputes

Partition Suits

Client Perspective: "I want my rightful share in the joint family property without unnecessary delays."

We file and defend partition suits for ancestral and jointly held properties, ensuring our clients receive their lawful share. We focus on equitable division through court or mutual settlement.

Key Aspects:
  • Partition by metes and bounds
  • Challenging unequal distribution
  • Family property settlement

Recovery of Money

Client Perspective: "Someone owes me money and is refusing to pay—can I recover it legally?"

We handle civil recovery cases for individuals and businesses facing financial loss due to unpaid loans, dishonoured cheques, or breach of agreements. Fast-track recovery through legal notice, civil suit, or summary proceedings under Order 37 CPC.

Applicable In:
  • Personal loans
  • Business credit recovery
  • Unpaid invoices/contracts

Injunction Matters

Client Perspective: "I need to stop someone from doing something that harms my rights or property."

We help clients seek temporary or permanent injunctions to prevent illegal construction, trespassing, sale of disputed property, or interference with personal rights. Injunctions protect your interests until the matter is fully resolved.

Types of Injunctions:
  • Temporary
  • Permanent
  • Mandatory

Specific Performance Cases

Client Perspective: "The other party backed out of our agreement—I want the court to enforce the contract."

When contracts related to property sale, business deals, or agreements are breached, we help clients seek specific performance orders to legally compel the defaulting party to fulfill their obligations.

Common Cases:
  • Sale agreement enforcement
  • Property transfer disputes
  • Real estate contract breaches

Succession and Inheritance Disputes

Client Perspective: "I want to claim my rightful inheritance or challenge an unfair will."

We offer legal assistance in inheritance disputes, succession certificate matters, and challenges to wills. Whether it's intestate succession (without a will) or execution of a will, we ensure your legal rights are upheld.

We Handle:
  • Legal heirship certificates
  • Will disputes
  • Hindu Succession Act cases
  • Probate and letters of administration
