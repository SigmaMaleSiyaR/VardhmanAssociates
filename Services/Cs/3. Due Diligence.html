Types of Due Diligence We Offer

Legal Due Diligence

Verification of company records, contracts, compliance history, and legal liabilities. Review of MoA, AoA, litigations, licenses, approvals, etc.

Financial Due Diligence

Analysis of financial statements, tax records, debt, and financial obligations. Ensures accuracy and transparency of financial data.

Secretarial Due Diligence

Review of board minutes, ROC filings, statutory registers, and corporate governance practices. Ensures compliance with Companies Act and SEBI regulations.

Tax Due Diligence

Assessment of tax liabilities, GST compliance, tax litigations, and risks.
