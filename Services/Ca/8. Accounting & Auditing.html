Auditing is the independent examination of your financial records, operations, or specific areas (like GST, stock, HR, etc.) to ensure accuracy, compliance, and internal control effectiveness.

Audit Type

Description

Statutory Audit

Mandatory for companies under the Companies Act. We verify your books and file audit reports with ROC.

Internal Audit

Helps detect fraud, errors, and inefficiencies. Recommended for mid-size to large businesses.

Review of GST records, reconciliation with GSTR-1/3B/9, and compliance checks.

Real-time review of financial transactions. Mostly used in banks or high-risk transactions.

Management/Operational Audit

Helps improve business processes, efficiency, and profitability.

Special Purpose Audit

Custom audits based on business needs like merger support, forensic audit, NGO grants, etc.
