📚 Documents Required for Property Valuation:

Clients must be ready with key documents for accurate and lawful valuation. These typically include:

  1. Property Title Deed / Sale Deed
  2. Property Tax Receipts
  3. Approved Building Plan / Layout Plan
  4. Encumbrance Certificate
  5. Mutation Records / Land Records
  6. Occupancy Certificate (for constructed properties)
  7. Previous Valuation Report (if any)
  8. Government Gazette Notification (if land acquisition is involved)

 

👨‍⚖️ Who Can Conduct a Property Valuation?

Valuation must be done by:

  • Registered Government Valuers
  • Chartered Engineers (in some cases)
  • Architects or Certified Surveyors
  • Legal Experts (to ensure legality of title & compliance)

Note: As legal professionals, we coordinate with authorized valuers to ensure that the report meets the legal purpose and is admissible in relevant authorities, banks, or courts.
