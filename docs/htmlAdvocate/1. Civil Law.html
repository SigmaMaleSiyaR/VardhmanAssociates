Partition Suits

Client Perspective: "I want my rightful share in the joint family property without unnecessary delays."

We file and defend partition suits for ancestral and jointly held properties, ensuring our clients receive their lawful share. We focus on equitable division through court or mutual settlement.

Key Aspects:

Partition by metes and bounds

Challenging unequal distribution

Family property settlement

Recovery of Money

Client Perspective: "Someone owes me money and is refusing to pay—can I recover it legally?"

We handle civil recovery cases for individuals and businesses facing financial loss due to unpaid loans, dishonoured cheques, or breach of agreements. Fast-track recovery through legal notice, civil suit, or summary proceedings under Order 37 CPC.

Applicable In:

Personal loans

Business credit recovery

Unpaid invoices/contracts

Injunction Matters

Client Perspective: "I need to stop someone from doing something that harms my rights or property."

We help clients seek temporary or permanent injunctions to prevent illegal construction, trespassing, sale of disputed property, or interference with personal rights. Injunctions protect your interests until the matter is fully resolved.

Types of Injunctions:

Temporary

Permanent

Mandatory
