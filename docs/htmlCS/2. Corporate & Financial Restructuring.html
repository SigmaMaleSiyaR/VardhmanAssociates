What is Corporate & Financial Restructuring?

It refers to the reorganization of a companys legal, ownership, operational, or financial structure with the objective of enhancing business efficiency, managing risks, complying with regulatory norms, or overcoming financial distress.

 

Types of Restructuring Services We Offer:

🔷 Corporate Restructuring

  • Mergers & Amalgamations
  • Demerger or Spin-off of Units
  • Change in Capital Structure (e.g., increase/reduction of share capital)
  • Conversion of Company Type (e.g., Private to Public or vice versa)
  • Buy-back of Shares
  • Change in Ownership Structure

 

🔷 Financial Restructuring

  • Debt Restructuring (negotiation with creditors, loan rescheduling)
  • Equity Restructuring (rights issue, bonus issue, preference share changes)
  • Settlement Agreements with Banks/Financial Institutions
  • Revival of Sick Companies
  • Insolvency & Bankruptcy Process Support

🔍 Regulatory Framework Involved

We ensure full compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including:

  • Companies Act, 2013 (for mergers, demergers, buy-backs, capital reduction, etc.)
  • Insolvency & Bankruptcy Code (IBC), 2016
  • SEBI Regulations (for listed companies)
  • Income Tax Act, 1961 (for tax-neutral restructuring)
  • FEMA Regulations (for foreign investments)
  • RBI Guidelines (for debt restructuring and banking compliance)

 

📜 Documentation & Filings Handled

  • Drafting of Restructuring Scheme / Business Transfer Agreements
  • Shareholder and Board Resolutions
  • Filing with Registrar of Companies (ROC)
  • Application filing with NCLT (National Company Law Tribunal)
  • Coordination with auditors, legal counsels, and valuation professionals
  • Support in stamp duty assessments and tax clearance certificates
