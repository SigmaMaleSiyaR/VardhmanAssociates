TYPES OF BUSINESS REGISTRATION / LICENSE

Comprehensive Guide to Business Registrations & Licenses in India

  • Business Entity Registration

Need: Legal identity for business

Purpose: Structure, compliance, taxation

Authority: MCA (Ministry of Corporate Affairs), Registrar of Firms

Types/Levels:

Sole Proprietorship

Partnership Firm (Registered/Unregistered)

LLP (Limited Liability Partnership)

Private Limited Company

One Person Company (OPC)

Public Limited Company

Who Needs This: Every new business

Who Is It Required For: Entrepreneurs, startups, firms, companies

  • GST Registration

Need: To collect and remit GST

Purpose: Indirect tax compliance

Authority: GSTN (Goods and Services Tax Network)

Types/Levels:

Regular Taxpayer

Composition Scheme

Casual Taxable Person

Input Service Distributor

Who Needs This: Turnover > Rs. 40 lakh (Rs. 20 lakh for services)

Who Is It Required For: Traders, manufacturers, service providers, e-commerce sellers

  • FSSAI Registration (Food License)

Need: Legal approval for food businesses

Purpose: Food safety and hygiene compliance

Authority: FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India)

Types/Levels:

Basic (Turnover < Rs. 12 lakh)

State License (Rs. 12 lakh - 20 crore)

Central License (> Rs. 20 crore or interstate)

Who Needs This: All food-related businesses

Who Is It Required For: Cafes, restaurants, food manufacturers, food trucks, cloud kitchens

  • Drug License

Need: Sale/manufacture of drugs

Purpose: Ensure regulated use and distribution

Authority: State Drug Control Department

Types/Levels:

Retail License

Wholesale License

Manufacturing License

Who Needs This: Pharmaceutical businesses

Who Is It Required For: Chemists, pharmacies, drug wholesalers, manufacturers

  • Fire Safety NOC

Need: Fire safety compliance

Purpose: Protection from fire hazards

Authority: Local Fire Department

Types/Levels:

Fire NOC (based on building type and area)

Who Needs This: Businesses with large premises or high occupancy

Who Is It Required For: Restaurants, factories, hospitals, educational institutions

  • Labour Registration

Need: Labour law compliance

Purpose: Workers’ rights and protection

Authority: State Labour Department

Types/Levels:

Contract Labour (CLRA)

Building & Construction Workers (BOCW)

Who Needs This: Labour-intensive businesses

Who Is It Required For: Contractors, builders, factories, service providers

  • Factory License

Need: To run a manufacturing unit

Purpose: Safety, health, and welfare of workers

Authority: Inspector of Factories (State)

Types/Levels:

Based on number of workers and power usage

Who Needs This: Manufacturing units

Who Is It Required For: Industrial and factory owners

  • EPF Registration

Need: Social security for employees

Purpose: Provident fund contribution

Authority: EPFO (Employees' Provident Fund Organisation)

Types/Levels:

Compulsory for establishments with 20+ employees

Who Needs This: Businesses employing staff

Who Is It Required For: Companies, organizations, NGOs

  • ESI Registration

Need: Medical benefits for employees

Purpose: Health insurance coverage

Authority: ESIC (Employees’ State Insurance Corporation)

Types/Levels:

Applicable for employees earning < Rs. 21,000/month

Who Needs This: Businesses with 10+ employees

Who Is It Required For: Employers in factories, offices, shops

  • MSME/Udyam Registration

Need: To avail government schemes

Purpose: Formal recognition as MSME

Authority: Udyam Registration Portal

Types/Levels:

Micro

Small

Medium (based on turnover & investment)

Who Needs This: Small & Medium businesses

Who Is It Required For: Traders, manufacturers

  • Pollution Control NOC

Need: To control industrial pollution

Purpose: Environmental compliance

Authority: State Pollution Control Board

Types/Levels:

Consent to Establish (CTE)

Consent to Operate (CTO)

Who Needs This: Industries causing pollution

Who Is It Required For: Factories, processing units

  • Import Export Code (IEC)

Need: For international trade

Purpose: Track and regulate imports/exports

Authority: DGFT (Director General of Foreign Trade)

Types/Levels:

One-time 10-digit code

Who Needs This: Businesses trading abroad

Who Is It Required For: Exporters and importers

  • ISI Certification

Need: Product quality assurance

Purpose: Mandatory for some products

Authority: BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards)

Types/Levels:

Based on product category

Who Needs This: Product manufacturers

Who Is It Required For: Electronics, appliances, cement manufacturers

  • ISO Certification

Need: To assure quality and standards

Purpose: Process & service certification

Authority: ISO Accredited Certification Bodies

Types/Levels:

ISO 9001, 14001, 22000, etc.

Who Needs This: Any growing business

Who Is It Required For: Manufacturers, service companies, exporters

  • Trademark Registration

Need: Brand protection

Purpose: Legal ownership of brand

Authority: Trademark Registry (IP India)

Types/Levels:

Wordmark, Logo, Sound, Color

Who Needs This: Branding entities

Who Is It Required For: Startups, established businesses, content creators

  • Copyright Registration

Need: Protection of creative work

Purpose: Legal rights over original content

Authority: Copyright Office

Types/Levels:

Literary, musical, artistic, software

Who Needs This: Creators and publishers

Who Is It Required For: Authors, musicians, software developers

  • Patent Registration

Need: To protect inventions

Purpose: Exclusive rights

Authority: Indian Patent Office

Types/Levels:

Utility, Design, Provisional/Complete

Who Needs This: Inventors, companies with R&D

Who Is It Required For: Tech startups, innovators

  • Shop & Establishment License

Need: State-wise regulation of commercial premises

Purpose: Employee welfare & legal recognition

Authority: State Labour Department

Types/Levels:

Varies by state and business type

Who Needs This: Every shop/office

Who Is It Required For: Retailers, freelancers, offices, cafes

  • SAN (Sanstha Aadhaar Number)

Need: For NGOs and voluntary organizations

Purpose: Government grants & recognition

Authority: NGO Darpan (NITI Aayog)

Types/Levels:

Unique SAN Number

Who Needs This: NGOs, Trusts, Societies

Who Is It Required For: Non-profit organizations

  • Central Excise Registration

Need: Production of excisable goods

Purpose: Excise duty collection (pre-GST)

Authority: CBIC (Central Board of Indirect Taxes & Customs)

Types/Levels:

Based on turnover & type of goods

Who Needs This: Select legacy industries

Who Is It Required For: Tobacco, alcohol, petroleum sectors

  • VAT & CST Registration

Need: Legacy sales tax system

Purpose: Tax on intra/interstate sales (Pre-GST)

Authority: State Commercial Tax Departments

Types/Levels:

VAT: Within state

CST: Interstate

Who Needs This: Still applicable in some sectors

Who Is It Required For: Petroleum dealers, alcohol traders

  • Fertilizer License

Need: Sale of fertilizers

Purpose: Ensure quality and regulation

Authority: State Agriculture Department

Types/Levels:

Retailer, Wholesaler, Manufacturer

Who Needs This: Agriculture input dealers

Who Is It Required For: Fertilizer shops, agri businesses
